PUPQC Student Assistant Widget Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable PUPQC Student Assistant chatbot widget. The maroon chat button appears in the bottom-right corner.

How to integrate:

<script src="https://mypupqcchatbot-production.up.railway.app/widget/student.js" async></script>

Features:

  • Floating maroon chat button with PUPQC branding
  • Admin-configured quick question options
  • Clean, responsive chat interface
  • Natural conversation responses (no technical jargon)
  • Typing indicators for better user experience
  • Mobile-friendly design that works on all devices
  • Powered by AI with uploaded document knowledge

Try it out:

Click the maroon chat button in the bottom-right corner. You'll see quick question options that admins have configured, or you can ask your own questions about PUPQC student services.

Sample questions you can ask:

  • "What are the requirements for Latin honors?"
  • "How do I enroll for the next semester?"
  • "What financial aid options are available?"
  • "Who is Jose Rizal?"
  • Or use the quick option buttons that appear when you open the chat

For website owners:

Simply copy the script tag above and paste it anywhere in your HTML. The widget will automatically appear on your site with full PUPQC Student Assistant functionality.
