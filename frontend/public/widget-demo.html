Student Support Chatbot Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable student chatbot widget. The chat button appears in the bottom-right corner.

How to integrate:

<script src="https://mypupqcchatbot-production.up.railway.app/widget/student.js"></script>

Features:

  • Floating chat button in bottom-right corner
  • Clean, responsive chat interface
  • Session persistence across page reloads
  • Source citations from uploaded documents
  • Typing indicators for better UX
  • Mobile-friendly design

Try it out:

Click the chat button in the bottom-right corner and ask questions about your uploaded student documents.

Sample questions:

  • "What are the graduation requirements?"
  • "When is the deadline for course registration?"
  • "How do I apply for financial aid?"
